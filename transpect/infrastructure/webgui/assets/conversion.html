Conversion/Checks

After the conversion has finished, please push the button “Ok” below to see the results (some scrolling may be necessary to see the button).

If the conversion finishes successfully, it doesn’t necessarily mean that the checking reports are ok.

Please check the report that is linked on the following page in any case and to react upon (at least) the messages marked in red.

Depending on the input file size and the conversion complexity, the process may take several minutes.
